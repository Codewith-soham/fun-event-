## Troubleshooting Quick Fixes

### Link won't copy?
- Tap the link text
- Hold and select "Copy"
- Or screenshot and share!

### Music won't play?
- Partner must click "Unmute"
- Browser blocks auto-play
- This is normal!

### Photo won't upload?
- Check file size (< 2MB)
- Use JPG or PNG
- Try compressing first

### Link too long?
- Use smaller files
- Compress images online
- Skip music (optional)
